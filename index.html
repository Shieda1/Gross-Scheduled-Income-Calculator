What is the gross scheduled income?
the gross scheduled income is the amount of cash generated by a commercial property with the assumption that the property is at full capacity.
How to calculate gross scheduled income?
To calculate gross scheduled income you should use the following formula:
gross scheduled income (GSI) = gross operating income + vacancy and credit loss
Why use gross scheduled income?
the gross scheduled income is used for measuring the sum of monthly rents for a particular property and it includes the collected, uncollected and potential rents. The gross scheduled income is used in real estate invrestments.
